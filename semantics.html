Summer

This young woman has power over bring sunshine and heat to Earth. She loves having fun with her friends, and doesn't let anything get her down. Most of the time she's full of energy, although at times she can get reckless and act without thinking. Despite this, she still has a big heart.

Secretly, she's a bit insecure over being the only season without a holiday.

Autumn

Autumn has powers relating to making plants...wilt or decay before Winter. He doesn't let his emotions get ahead of him too much, but he's really bad at talking to people and comes off as insensitive. He means well, though.

He has a debilitating weakness to attractive women. "Winter, I know you wrote this."

Winter

Winter, or Old Man Winter, has power over cold, snow and ice. Physically and mentally the oldest of the four, he believes that he should be the leader. Of course, due to his loud and rude nature, no one else thinks so. Despite his jerkish behavior, he does care about his three friends.

He once dressed as Santa Claus to make Spring happy, though he'll never admit it.

Spring

The youngest and most innocent of the four, this little girl has the powers to make flowers bloom. Because of her age, she believes that people underestimate her, and wishes to prove her strength to everyone, with varying levels of success...

She's a little shy when meeting new people, but immediately perks up when it comes to flowers.
